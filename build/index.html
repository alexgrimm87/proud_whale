Let me tell you how it works:

Research

It is really important to understand yours business and to know what makes yours business differ from other companies and make everything work well. Our analyst would find everything we need to make your Site look fabulous.

Prototype

The Prototype is the first step of creating, it doesn’t have the design, but but it is the simple way to find out where the pages would be, how they will work and where will be the information.

Design

After the prototyping - the designer will make the design, on this step you will know how your Site will look like. If you will need to change something - we will make it. It called Edits. You will have 2 free circles of Edits, that’s how you can be sure that we will make everything exactly how you want.

Front End Developing

Our Front End Developer would put the Design into your Site. It is already the half of the way. On this step you will see how your Site would look like in the Web and how it would work with the Design.
On this step you also will have 2 circles of Edits for free.

Back End Developing

And now Back End Developer will make Administration panel, payment system, Google Analytics, E-mail delivery, security system, itc.

The Hosting

And finally we will put your Site on Hosting (it’s like virtual house for your Site). And after that everyone would have opportunity visit the Site.

Filling

Everything well done, and here it's last touch - you need to fill your Site with goods and products, write descriptions and so on. For the extra pay - we can do it for you.

Education

You will have 2 hours of consultation, we will teach you how to use your Site properly absolutely free.

This is it, your Site is Done!
